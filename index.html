About Me

Hi, I'm Lindsay. An Atlanta native, I made my way to the Lone Star State to attend Southern Methodist University in Dallas, TX. While there, I studied music, advertising, and graphic design. After graduating in 2016, I've worked at Core Publishing LLC as the associate art director for our publications Dallas Hotel Magazine, Denver Hotel Magazine, and Houston Hotel Magazine .


I am currently enrolled in SMU's coding bootcamp program, after which I hope to pair my graphic design skills with front end development skills to create websites and applications that are equally beautiful and functional. Check out my work here or on GitHub, and feel free to get in touch - I'd love to hear from you!
